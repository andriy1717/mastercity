Your People's Saga

City Rebuilders is a turn-by-turn race to grow a shattered settlement into a legendary capital. Each civilization arrives with its own talents (Roman accountants, Viking hunters, Mongol smiths, Slavic farmers), but everyone faces the same pressures: scarce seasons, prowling raiders, and rival leaders chasing the same dream. Guide your people from the Wood Age to the Modern Age, hold off every threat, and finish a city that will outlive every storyteller.

Victory Condition

🎯 The Goal: Build the Monument

Win by building the Monument in the Modern Age. This ultimate structure becomes available once you've built enough advanced buildings and have the resources to afford it.

Quick Start

Start with basic resources and a few soldiers. Use Moves each turn to gather resources, build structures, and advance through the Wood → Stone → Modern ages. Buildings you create stay forever and provide permanent bonuses.

Each turn you get 3 Moves to spend on actions like gathering resources, building structures, training soldiers, or trading. Unused Moves can be saved in a small bank for later. Your civilization bonus and seasonal effects influence how many resources you gather.

How It Works

⚙️ Core Mechanics

📦 Resources & Gathering

4 Main Resources: Wood, Rock, Metal, Food

Golden Coins: Earned from buildings and trades

Spend 1 Move to gather any resource. Your buildings and civilization bonus affect how much you get.

🏗️ Building & Upgrading

Build: Pay resources to construct new buildings (no Move cost)

Upgrade: Spend 1 Move + coins to increase building output

Each building can only be built once, but upgrades make them more powerful.

⚔️ Military & Combat

Soldiers: Defend against raids and launch wars

Defense: Higher defense % = better protection from tribal raids

Upkeep: Armies now eat twice as much Food (≈1 per 2 soldiers) and a little Coin (≈1 per 10 soldiers) each turn.

💰 Trading & Diplomacy

Bank Trading: Sell 3 resources for 1 coin, or buy 1 resource for 2 coins

Player Trading: Make direct deals with other players

Visitors: Send traders, spies, or robbers to other players

Turn Sequence

1. Income: Collect coins from buildings
2. Upkeep: Feed your soldiers
3. Actions: Spend your 3 Moves on gathering, building, trading, or military
4. Season: Time advances, seasonal effects and events occur

Advancement Path

🏗️ Progression: The Three Ages

Advance automatically when you build enough buildings in your current age. Progress is permanent - you never go backwards, and old buildings keep working.

🌲 Wood Age

Starting Age: Basic buildings and simple defenses.

Fields (Food) Sawmills (Wood) Markets (Coins) Barracks (Soldiers)

Advance: Build 2 different Wood Age buildings

🏰 Stone Age

Mid Game: Stronger buildings and better defenses.

Quarries (Rock) Town Centers (Coins) Stone Walls (Defense) Armories (Army)

Advance: Build 3 different Stone Age buildings

⚙️ Modern Age

End Game: Industrial buildings and the Monument.

Factories (Metal) Banks (Coins) Defense Grids Monument (Victory!)

Victory: Build the Monument to win

Building Strategy

Start with 2 random buildings visible per age. Build your first structure in a new age to see all other options. Focus on resource production early, then build defenses and the Monument.

Game Systems

✨ Key Features

🌍 Seasons & Events

Seasons change every few turns and affect resource gathering. Spring boosts food, Summer favors wood and metal, Autumn provides balanced gathering, and Winter slows most production. Random events can provide bonuses or challenges.

🏛️ Civilizations

Romans: +15% Rock, +1 coin/turn, -10% Metal
Vikings: +20% Food, -5% Wood
Mongols: +10% Metal, -10% Rock
Slavs: +10% Wood & Food, -15% Metal, -1 coin/turn

⚔️ Military & War

Defense: Soldiers + walls protect against tribal raids. Low defense = lost resources or destroyed buildings.

Go To War: Launch wars to plunder resources. You may go to war at most once every 6 months (in‑game). Larger armies bring back larger loot, but success rates are lower and casualties are higher than before.

Upkeep: Armies consume double Food and a small amount of Coins each turn, scaling with army size.

Presentation: Army strength is shown with themed size names (e.g., Small Band, Warband, Cohort, Legion) based on your civilization and age.

Mercenaries: Pay 20 coins for a guaranteed end‑of‑season raid against a chosen target (mercenaries keep 30% of the spoils).

🕵️ Diplomacy & Visitors

Traders: Both players gain coins if accepted
Spies: Reveal enemy defense and resources
Robbers: Steal coins/resources if accepted
Cost: 10 coins + 1 Move, once per season

🔧 Building Upgrades

Spend 1 Move + coins to upgrade buildings for better output. Success rate decreases with each level (100% → 90% → 80%, etc.). 5% chance for critical success that grants +2 levels instead of +1.

Good luck, and may your city prosper!
